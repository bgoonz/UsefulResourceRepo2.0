about

Free Code Camp Sheridan is a local chapter of an open source community that helps you learn to code. You can work through coding challenges, build projects (including improving this website), and earn certifications with people right here in Sheridan so you can learn together!


So far, hundreds of people have gotten software engineering jobs after completing these coding challenges. We offer four free verified certifications: front end development, back end development, data visualization, and full stack development.


Once you finish the first three certifications, you’ll get to build a series of solutions for nonprofits. You’ll work in pairs, under the supervision of a volunteer project manager and a stakeholder from the nonprofit. Our community has done nearly a million dollars worth of pro-bono coding for nonprofits so far.


How long does it take? It takes about 2,080 hours to earn all four of our certifications. This translates into one year of full-time coding. We’re completely self-paced though, so you can take as long as you need. Our curriculum, nonprofit projects, and verified certifications are all free.


Sign up to our mailing list or our facebook group page above to find out when our next meetup is and join for free!
