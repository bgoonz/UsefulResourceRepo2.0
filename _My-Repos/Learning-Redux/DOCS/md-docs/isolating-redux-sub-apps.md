It's best not to mix this approach with standard Redux reducer composition. For typical web apps, stick with reducer composition. For ‚Äúproduct hubs‚Äù, ‚Äúdashboards‚Äù, or enterprise software that groups disparate tools into a unified package, give the sub-app approach a try.

The sub-app approach is also useful for large teams that are divided by product or feature verticals. These teams can ship sub-apps independently or in combination with an enclosing ‚Äúapp shell‚Äù.
